we are SOLUTIONS pdm

SOLUTIONS pdm is a fully integrated professional services organization that specializes in managing the planning, design, construction, and move-in process for corporate owners and users of office, retail, and multi-family property types.

What We Do

SOLUTIONS pdm assists corporate clients throughout the planning, design, construction and move-in process, reducing the inherent risk associated with construction, reducing project cost 5 -10%, and by reducing our client’s time commitment 20 -30 hours per week.

Our mission is to deliver an unparalled level of service to each of our clients by subscribing to the following CORE principles upon which our company was founded:


ASSIST our clients throughout the entire project delivery process from inception to move-in, all the while keeping our clients informed

SERVE as your professional advocate, always promoting efficiency, tightening standards, promoting educated spending and heightening urgency.

CONTROL the inherent risk associated with the construction process, lowering project cost 5-10% and returning 20-30 hours per week of our client’s time commitment in the process


SOLUTIONS pdm manages the design and construction process by tightening standards, promoting educated spending, and heightening urgency. By implementing controls and execution of our project delivery process, we save our clients a considerable amount of money, and perhaps more importantly, a substantial amount of time, frustration and miscommunication between the multiple parties involved.

SOLUTIONS pdm leverages our in-depth knowledge and combined 40 years of industry experience to develop individualized budgets and schedules that maximize savings for our clients. Our background in the design and construction industry are key essentials in developing the “Project Team” including architects, contractors, furniture, telecommunications cabling and other specialty vendors that will ultimately make a successful project.
